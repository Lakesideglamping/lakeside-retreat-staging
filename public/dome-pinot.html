The Ultimate Romantic Escape

Welcome to Dome Pinot, Lakeside Retreat's flagship experience. Our largest and most spacious luxury dome offers an unforgettable 50sqm open living space, where the serenity of nature combines seamlessly with five-star accommodation comforts. Purposely built for couples seeking the ultimate romantic escape, Dome Pinot features panoramic floor-to-ceiling windows showcasing breathtaking views of Lake Dunstan and the surrounding Central Otago mountains.
