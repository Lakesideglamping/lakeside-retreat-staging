Our Story

The journey behind Central Otago's premier sustainable luxury retreat

Welcome to Our Dream

We're Stephen and Sandy, and Lakeside Retreat is the culmination of our dream to create something truly special in one of New Zealand's most beautiful regions. What started as a vision to share the magic of Central Otago has grown into New Zealand's first energy-positive luxury accommodation.

When we first discovered this piece of land on the shores of Lake Dunstan, we knew we'd found something extraordinary. The panoramic views of the lake and surrounding mountains, the proximity to world-class wineries, and the access to the famous Otago Rail Trail made it the perfect location for what we had in mind.

Building Sustainably

From the beginning, sustainability wasn't just a buzzword for us - it was a core principle. We invested in a commercial-grade 16.72kW solar system with 38 Helios panels and Signergy battery storage. This system doesn't just power our retreat; it generates more energy than we use, feeding the surplus back into the national grid.

Our geodesic domes were chosen not just for their striking appearance, but for their efficiency. The dome shape provides excellent insulation and structural integrity, while the panoramic windows connect guests with the stunning landscape without compromising on comfort.
