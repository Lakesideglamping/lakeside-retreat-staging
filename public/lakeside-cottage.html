Your Family Lakeside Escape

Lakeside Cottage offers the perfect family getaway with direct access to Lake Dunstan's crystal-clear waters. This spacious cottage sleeps up to 6 guests across two bedrooms, making it ideal for families or groups of friends. Enjoy morning swims, kayaking adventures, and evening BBQs on your private deck overlooking the lake. As our only pet-friendly accommodation, your furry family members are welcome too!
