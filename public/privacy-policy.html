Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information

Effective date: 1 January 2026
Last updated: 28 February 2026

Lakeside Retreat ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store and disclose your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and its Information Privacy Principles (IPPs).

1. Who We Are

Lakeside Retreat is an accommodation provider located at 96 Smiths Way, Mount Pisa, Cromwell 9310, New Zealand. We operate three luxury accommodations: Dome Pinot, Dome Rosé and Lakeside Cottage on the shores of Lake Dunstan.

Privacy Officer: Sandy Douglas
Email: info@lakesideretreat.co.nz
Phone: 021 368 682

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly when you:

  • Make a booking — full name, email address, phone number, number of guests, check-in/check-out dates, accommodation preference
  • Make a payment — payment is processed by Stripe; we do not store your credit card details. We receive a payment confirmation reference only
  • Contact us — name, email, phone number and the content of your message
  • Leave a review — name, location and your review content
  • Visit our website — IP address, browser type, pages visited, visit duration (via Google Analytics with anonymised IP addresses)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and manage your accommodation booking
  • Send booking confirmations and check-in instructions
  • Process payments through our secure payment provider (Stripe)
  • Synchronise bookings with channel managers (Uplisting) for availability management across Airbnb and Booking.com
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website and guest experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We will not use your information for any purpose other than those listed above without your consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your personal information only with:

  • Stripe — our payment processor, to securely handle your payment. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/nz/privacy
  • Uplisting — our channel manager, to synchronise your booking across platforms and prevent double-bookings
  • Airbnb / Booking.com — if your booking originates from one of these platforms, your information is shared according to their respective privacy policies
  • Google Analytics — anonymised website usage data to help us understand how visitors use our site

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third parties for marketing purposes.

5. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss or misuse:

  • All data transmitted to and from our website is encrypted using SSL/TLS (HTTPS)
  • Payment information is handled exclusively by Stripe and never stored on our servers
  • Our website is protected by security headers, rate limiting and CSRF protection
  • Administrative access is secured with multi-factor authentication
  • Booking data is stored on secure, hosted infrastructure

We retain your booking information for up to 7 years for accounting and legal compliance. Contact enquiry data is retained for 2 years, after which it is deleted.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Essential cookies — required for the booking system and security (CSRF tokens)
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, to understand website usage (anonymised)

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Declining non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to browse our website, though some booking features may be affected.

7. Your Rights Under the Privacy Act 2020

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct any inaccurate or outdated information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@lakesideretreat.co.nz. We will respond within 20 working days as required by the Act.

8. International Visitors

Our services are primarily provided in New Zealand. If you are visiting from overseas, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and stored in New Zealand. By making a booking or contacting us, you consent to this transfer. New Zealand has been recognised by the European Commission as providing an adequate level of data protection.

9. Children's Privacy

Our booking system is not designed for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Bookings must be made by an adult.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Complaints

If you believe we have breached your privacy, please contact us first at info@lakesideretreat.co.nz. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner:

  • Website: privacy.org.nz
  • Phone: 0800 803 909
