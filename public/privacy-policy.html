Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: March 2026

Lakeside Retreat ("we", "us", or "our") operates luxury glamping and accommodation services at 96 Smiths Way, Mount Pisa, Cromwell 9310, New Zealand. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 (NZPA).

1. Information We Collect

When you make a booking or enquiry, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name — to identify you and personalise your stay
  • Email address — to send booking confirmations and pre-arrival information
  • Phone number — to contact you regarding your booking or in case of emergencies
  • Postal address — if required for invoicing purposes
  • Booking details — dates, accommodation preferences, number of guests, and any special requirements you share with us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Processing and confirming your accommodation booking
  • Communicating with you before, during, and after your stay
  • Sending check-in instructions and property information
  • Responding to enquiries and requests
  • Improving our services and guest experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

We will not use your personal information for marketing purposes unless you have given us explicit consent to do so.

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you make a payment:

  • Your credit or debit card details are entered directly into Stripe's secure payment form
  • We do not store, access, or retain your full card number, CVV, or other sensitive card details on our systems
  • Stripe may retain certain transaction information in accordance with their own privacy policy
  • Security bond pre-authorisations are also processed through Stripe

For more information on how Stripe handles your data, please visit Stripe's Privacy Policy.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (measurement ID: G-M6TC9MB5CR) to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search terms used to find us
  • General geographic location (country/region level)
  • Browser type, device type, and screen resolution
  • Anonymous usage statistics

This data is aggregated and anonymised. It does not personally identify you. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic website functionality and the booking system
  • Analytics cookies — used by Google Analytics to understand website usage patterns

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • Stripe — for secure payment processing
  • Google — through Google Analytics for anonymised website usage data
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect our legal rights

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Booking records — retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting purposes, as required by New Zealand law
  • Enquiry correspondence — retained for up to 2 years, then deleted
  • Analytics data — automatically anonymised and aggregated by Google Analytics after 14 months

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure. This includes:

  • Using HTTPS encryption across our entire website
  • Processing payments through PCI-DSS certified Stripe infrastructure
  • Limiting access to personal information to authorised personnel only
  • Regularly reviewing our data handling practices

9. Your Rights Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020

Under the NZPA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal retention requirements
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner if you believe your privacy has been breached

We will respond to any privacy-related request within 20 working days, as required by the Act.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as booking platforms, local attractions, or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, wish to exercise your privacy rights, or have a concern about how we handle your personal information, please contact us:

  • Email: info@lakesideretreat.co.nz
  • Address: Lakeside Retreat, 96 Smiths Way, Mount Pisa, Cromwell 9310, New Zealand

You may also contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at www.privacy.org.nz if you wish to make a complaint.
