The Perfect Weekend Escape from Queenstown

Swap the crowds for calm lakeside luxury, just 45 minutes from Queenstown

If you are planning a weekend getaway from Queenstown and looking for something beyond the busy streets and bustling restaurants, Lakeside Retreat in Cromwell offers a completely different pace. Set on the shores of Lake Dunstan, our luxury glamping property sits right between Queenstown and Wanaka, giving you the best of both worlds without the premium price tag or the noise.

Why Choose Cromwell Over Queenstown

Queenstown is a spectacular destination, but it has also become one of the busiest tourist centres in New Zealand. During peak season, the streets are crowded, parking can be difficult, and accommodation prices climb steeply. Cromwell, just 45 minutes east along the stunning Kawarau Gorge, offers a genuine alternative to Queenstown accommodation that many visitors overlook.

Choosing Cromwell accommodation near Queenstown does not mean missing out. It means gaining an authentic Central Otago experience. You are in the heart of New Zealand's premier wine-growing region, surrounded by mountain views and golden tussock landscape, with Lake Dunstan at your doorstep. It is the kind of place where you genuinely unwind.

Getting Here: A Scenic Drive You Will Enjoy

The drive from Queenstown to Lakeside Retreat is one of the most scenic routes in the South Island. You will head east along State Highway 6, passing through the dramatic Kawarau Gorge, past the original AJ Hackett Bungy bridge, and alongside the turquoise Kawarau River. Many of our guests tell us the drive alone was a highlight of their trip.

What You Will Love About Staying Here

Lakeside Retreat is not just an alternative to Queenstown accommodation. It is a destination in its own right. Here is what draws guests back year after year.

Lake Dunstan

Swimming, kayaking, paddleboarding and fishing in crystal-clear waters. Direct lake access from the Lakeside Cottage.

Wine Country

Over 30 world-class wineries within 15 minutes. Central Otago produces some of the finest Pinot Noir on earth.

Cycle Trails

The Otago Rail Trail is just 300 metres from your door. The Lake Dunstan Cycle Trail is a short drive away.

Mountain Views

Wake up to panoramic views of the Pisa Range and surrounding mountains from your dome or cottage.

Stargazing

Minimal light pollution means you can see the Milky Way clearly. Our domes have skylights designed for stargazing from bed.

Private Spas

Both geodesic domes feature private outdoor spas with mountain views. Soak under the stars after a day of exploring.

Queenstown Day Trips Made Easy

One of the biggest advantages of staying between Queenstown and Wanaka is that you can easily visit either town for a day of adventure, then return to the serenity of Lakeside Retreat each evening. Many of our guests prefer this approach over staying in Queenstown itself.

Head into Queenstown for a morning bungy jump at the Kawarau Bridge (you will drive right past it), take the Skyline Gondola for panoramic views over Lake Wakatipu, or spend the afternoon jet boating on the Shotover River. By late afternoon, you are back at the retreat, relaxing in your private spa with a glass of local Pinot Noir while the sun sets over the mountains.

Local Tip

Leave Queenstown by 4pm to catch golden hour light through the Kawarau Gorge on your drive back. The afternoon light on the river and cliffs is genuinely breathtaking, and most tourists heading in the opposite direction will miss it entirely.

Wanaka is even closer at just 30 minutes. Visit the famous Wanaka Tree, explore Puzzling World with the kids, or tackle the Roy's Peak track for one of the most photographed viewpoints in New Zealand. In winter, the Cardrona and Treble Cone ski fields are easily accessible from both Wanaka and Cromwell.

Better Value, Better Experience

Accommodation in central Queenstown comes at a premium. A standard hotel room in peak season can easily exceed $400 per night, and many lack the character and privacy that make a holiday feel special. At Lakeside Retreat, you get far more for your money.

Our Lakeside Cottage starts from $295 per night and sleeps up to six, making it excellent value for families. It is pet-friendly, comes with its own kitchen, and has direct access to Lake Dunstan. For couples seeking a romantic weekend getaway from Queenstown, our geodesic domes offer a truly unique experience that no hotel in Queenstown can replicate, complete with private spas, panoramic skylights, and complete seclusion.

The cost savings extend beyond accommodation as well. Dining in Cromwell and the surrounding wine region is more affordable than Queenstown, and you will not need to pay for parking or deal with the markup that comes with a heavily touristed town centre.
