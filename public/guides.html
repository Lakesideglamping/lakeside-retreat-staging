Featured Guide

The Ultimate Central Otago Wine Trail

Central Otago is world-renowned for its Pinot Noir, and with over 30 wineries within 15 minutes of Lakeside Retreat, you're perfectly positioned to explore. Our curated wine trail takes you through the best cellar doors, from boutique family operations to award-winning estates. We recommend starting at Carrick Winery for their stunning views, then heading to Mt Difficulty for their exceptional Pinot Noir, and finishing at Wooing Tree for their unique underground cellar.
