Cycling & Rail Trail

Otago Rail Trail

300m from your door

New Zealand's original Great Ride. Access the Cromwell to Clyde section directly from Lakeside Retreat. Bike rentals and luggage transfers available in Cromwell.

Lake Dunstan Trail

5 min drive to trailhead

A spectacular 55km trail connecting Cromwell to Clyde along the lake edge, featuring tunnels, bridges, and stunning lake views.

Water Activities

Lake Dunstan Swimming

On-site access

Crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming in summer. The Lakeside Cottage has direct lake access, or use the nearby public beaches.

Kayaking & Paddleboarding

Equipment hire in Cromwell

Explore the lake at your own pace. Calm waters make it perfect for beginners. Rentals available from Cromwell Water Sports.

Day Trips

Queenstown Adventures

55 min drive

The adventure capital of New Zealand. Bungy jumping, jet boating, skiing, gondola rides, and world-class dining. Return to peaceful Lakeside Retreat each evening.

Wanaka & Surrounds

30 min drive

Visit the famous Wanaka Tree, explore Puzzling World, hike to Roy's Peak, or simply enjoy the relaxed lakeside atmosphere.

Historic Arrowtown

40 min drive

Charming gold rush town with boutique shops, excellent cafes, and beautiful autumn colours. Don't miss the Chinese Settlement heritage area.
