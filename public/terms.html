1. Bookings and Payment

All bookings are subject to availability and are confirmed only upon receipt of full payment or a valid payment authorisation.

  • All prices are quoted in New Zealand Dollars (NZD) and are inclusive of GST (Goods and Services Tax at 15%)
  • Payment is processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your credit or debit card details
  • A booking confirmation will be sent to your email address upon successful payment
  • By completing a booking, you confirm that all information provided is accurate and that you are authorised to use the payment method

2. Cancellation and Refund Policy

We understand plans can change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

Cancellation Notice Refund
7 or more days before check-in Full refund (100%)
3 to 6 days before check-in 50% refund
Less than 3 days before check-in No refund
  • Cancellations must be made in writing via email to info@lakesideretreat.co.nz
  • The cancellation date is determined by when we receive your written notice
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method and may take 5–10 business days to appear
  • No-shows are treated as a cancellation with less than 3 days notice

3. Security Bond

$300 Refundable Security Bond

A $300 NZD security bond is pre-authorised on your credit or debit card at the time of booking. This is not a charge — it is a temporary hold to cover any potential damages, excessive cleaning, or breach of property rules.

  • The pre-authorisation is processed through Stripe
  • If no issues arise, the hold is released within 48 hours after checkout
  • If deductions are necessary, we will notify you with an itemised breakdown before processing any charge

4. Check-In and Check-Out

  • Check-in: from 3:00 PM
  • Check-out: by 10:00 AM
  • Self check-in instructions will be provided via email prior to your arrival
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and is subject to availability. Additional charges may apply

5. Accommodation Types and Guest Restrictions

Glamping Domes — Dome Pinot and Dome Rosé

  • Maximum occupancy: 2 guests per dome
  • Dome Pinot and Dome Rosé are strictly adults-only accommodations. Children are not permitted
  • Pets are not permitted in the glamping domes

Lakeside Cottage

  • Maximum occupancy: 6 guests
  • Children are welcome at the Lakeside Cottage
  • Lakeside Cottage is pet-friendly with prior approval. You must notify us at the time of booking if you intend to bring a pet
  • A $25 per pet, per night cleaning fee applies
  • Pets must be supervised at all times and must not be left unattended in the cottage
  • Any damage caused by pets will be deducted from the security bond

6. Property Rules

To ensure a comfortable and safe experience for all guests, the following rules apply:

  • No smoking — All accommodations and indoor areas are strictly non-smoking. Smoking is permitted in designated outdoor areas only
  • Quiet hours — Please observe quiet hours between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM out of respect for other guests and neighbouring properties
  • No parties or events — Our accommodations are not suitable for parties, functions, or gatherings beyond the maximum occupancy
  • Rubbish — Please dispose of all rubbish in the bins provided before checkout
  • Fires — Open fires are not permitted anywhere on the property. Use only the provided fire pits and BBQ facilities
  • Parking — Please park in designated areas only

7. Maximum Occupancy

The maximum number of guests must not exceed the limits specified for each accommodation type:

  • Dome Pinot: 2 guests (adults only)
  • Dome Rosé: 2 guests (adults only)
  • Lakeside Cottage: 6 guests

Exceeding the maximum occupancy is a breach of these Terms and may result in immediate termination of your booking without refund.
