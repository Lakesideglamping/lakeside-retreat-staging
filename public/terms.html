Terms & Conditions

Booking terms, cancellation policy and house rules

Effective date: 1 January 2026
Last updated: 28 February 2026

By making a booking at Lakeside Retreat ("we", "us", "our"), you ("the guest") agree to these terms and conditions. Please read them carefully before completing your reservation.

1. Booking and Payment

  • All bookings are confirmed upon receipt of full payment
  • Payments are securely processed through Stripe. We do not store your credit card details
  • All prices are in New Zealand Dollars (NZD) and include GST (15%) as required by New Zealand law
  • Prices are subject to change without notice, but confirmed bookings will be honoured at the rate quoted

2. Accommodation Rates

  • Dome Pinot: From $530/night (2 guests)
  • Dome Rosé: From $510/night (2 guests)
  • Lakeside Cottage: From $295/night (up to 4 guests, additional guests $100/person/night, max 6)
  • Pets (Lakeside Cottage only): $25/pet/night

Security Bond

  • A $300 NZD refundable security bond is pre-authorised on your payment card at the time of booking
  • This is a hold only — the amount is not charged to your account
  • The bond is automatically released 48 hours after checkout, provided no damage claim is made
  • In the event of damage, excess cleaning or breach of house rules, part or all of the bond may be claimed
  • If a claim is made, you will be notified by email with details

Children Policy

  • Dome Pinot and Dome Rosé are strictly adults-only (18+)
  • No children of any age are permitted in the dome accommodations
  • If you arrive with children at a dome booking, you will be asked to leave immediately and no refund will be given. No exceptions.
  • Lakeside Cottage welcomes children and families

3. Minimum Stay

  • Standard: 2-night minimum stay
  • Peak season (December–February, public holidays): 3-night minimum stay

4. Cancellation Policy

  • 14+ days before check-in: Full refund
  • 7–14 days before check-in: 50% refund
  • Less than 7 days before check-in: No refund

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days. To request a cancellation, email info@lakesideretreat.co.nz or call 021 368 682.

5. Check-In and Check-Out

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM onwards
  • Check-out: 10:00 AM
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be available on request, subject to availability
  • Self check-in instructions will be emailed prior to your arrival

6. Guest Responsibilities

  • The maximum number of guests must not exceed the limit for the booked accommodation
  • Guests are responsible for any damage to the property or its contents during their stay
  • Any damage or breakage must be reported to us promptly
  • The cost of repairs or replacement for damage caused by guests will be charged accordingly
  • Guests must comply with the adults-only policy for dome accommodations

7. House Rules

  • No smoking inside any accommodation. Smoking is permitted in designated outdoor areas only
  • Pets are welcome at Lakeside Cottage only, by prior arrangement. A pet fee of $25/pet/night applies
  • Noise: Please be considerate of neighbours and other guests. Quiet hours are 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM
  • Parties/events: Not permitted without prior written approval
  • Fires: Open fires are not permitted. BBQ facilities are provided at Lakeside Cottage
  • Lake access: Use of kayaks and lake access is at your own risk. Children must be supervised at all times near the water

8. Liability and Insurance

  • Lakeside Retreat takes no responsibility for loss, theft or damage to personal belongings during your stay
  • We are not liable for any injury incurred while using the property's facilities, including the private spas, lake access and kayaks
  • Guests are encouraged to have their own travel insurance

Nothing in these terms limits your rights under the New Zealand Consumer Guarantees Act 1993.

9. Force Majeure

We shall not be held liable for failure to perform our obligations due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to: natural disasters, severe weather, pandemics, government restrictions or utility failures. In such cases, we will offer a full credit for a future stay or a full refund at the guest's choice.

10. Privacy

Your personal information is collected and handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. By making a booking, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in our Privacy Policy.

11. Bookings via Third Parties

Bookings made through Airbnb, Booking.com or other third-party platforms are subject to the terms and conditions of those platforms in addition to our house rules. In the event of a conflict, the third-party platform's cancellation policy will apply for bookings made through that platform.

12. Modifications to Bookings

  • Date changes are subject to availability and must be requested at least 7 days before the original check-in date
  • If the modified booking results in a higher rate, the difference must be paid at the time of modification
  • If the modified booking results in a lower rate, the difference will be refunded
