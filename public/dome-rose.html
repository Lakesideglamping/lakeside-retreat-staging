Romance in Wine Country

Dome Rosé offers an intimate 40sqm retreat perfectly positioned among the vineyards of Central Otago. Named after the region's famous rosé wines, this romantic dome features stunning views of the surrounding mountains and vineyard rows. With your own private outdoor spa, you can soak under the stars while enjoying a glass of local wine. The perfect escape for couples seeking tranquility and romance in New Zealand's premier wine region.
